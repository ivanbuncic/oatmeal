The Ultimate Porridge: The Last Recipe You'll Ever Need

Published: 7th October 2024

Are you on the hunt for a breakfast that's not only nutritious but also packed with flavour? Well, look no further! This is the last porridge recipe you'll ever need. This savoury delight is brimming with whole grains, fresh veggies, and proteins—perfect for kick-starting your day with gusto, and best of all, it requires minimal cooking effort.

Ingredients (for 1 serving):

  • Dry Ingredients (approximately 150g total):
    • Wholegrain porridge oats
      • AH Organic Wholegrain Porridge Oats
    • Chia seeds
      • AH Organic Chia Seeds
    • Flaxseeds
    • Buckwheat
      • Organic Buckwheat
  • Fresh Ingredients:
    • 50g red bell pepper, diced
      • Red Bell Pepper
    • 5 cherry tomatoes, halved
      • Cherry Tomatoes
    • 5 cubes of white cheese with herbs
      • Greek White Cheese Cubes with Herbs
    • 5 pitted olives
      • Pitted Olives
    • 50g smoked bacon strips
      • AH Organic Smoked Bacon Strips

Method:

  1. Preparing the Porridge:

    • Place the dry mix of wholegrain oats, chia seeds, flaxseeds, and buckwheat into a large bowl.
    • Boil 300 ml of water. (Time to put that kettle to good use!)
    • Pour the boiling water over the dry ingredients.
    • Give it a good stir to make sure everything is nicely combined.
    • Cover the bowl with a plate or lid, and let it soak for 15 minutes. This allows the grains to soften and the flavours to meld. Perfect for those who prefer to let breakfast make itself!

  2. Cooking the Bacon:

    • While your porridge is soaking up all that goodness, heat a frying pan over medium heat.
    • Add the smoked bacon strips and cook until they're delightfully crispy. (Resist the temptation to nibble—remember, sharing is caring!)

  3. Preparing the Fresh Ingredients:

    • Dice the red bell pepper.
    • Halve the cherry tomatoes.
    • No need to cook these; they'll add a fresh crunch and vibrant flavour to your porridge.

  4. Assembling the Dish:

    • Once the porridge has soaked and reached a lovely, creamy consistency, give it another stir.
    • Spoon the porridge into your favourite bowl. (We all have one, don't we?)
    • Top with the crispy bacon strips.
    • Scatter the diced red bell pepper, cherry tomatoes, and pitted olives over the top.
    • Finish off with the white cheese cubes with herbs. The cheese will soften slightly from the warmth—simply delightful!

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Your ultimate porridge is ready to be devoured. Grab a spoon and tuck in before someone else gets ideas!
